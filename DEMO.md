# SafeMGM Demo Guide

Use these queries during the live presentation to showcase the platform's intelligence and real-time enrichment capabilities.

## 🎙️ Recommended Demo Script

### 1. The Statistical Deep Dive
**Query:** *"What are the most common 911 calls in Montgomery?"*
- **Why:** Shows the AI parsing the local CSV data and citing specific numbers.
- **Expected Insight:** AI should mention 'DISTURBANCE' or 'THEFT' with exact counts from the dataset.

### 2. Neighborhood Intelligence (Map + Data)
**Query:** *"Tell me about public safety trends near downtown Montgomery."*
- **Why:** Triggers neighborhood-specific filtering and geospatial context.
- **Expected Insight:** AI discusses trends while citing specific incident types seen on the map.

### 3. Real-time News Enrichment (Bright Data)
**Query:** *"Are there any recent safety concerns or community alerts in Montgomery?"*
- **Why:** Showcases the Bright Data SERP API fetching live headlines.
- **Expected Insight:** AI response includes 📰 prefixed facts from recent local news reports.

### 4. Actionable Advice
**Query:** *"I'm visiting the Garden District this weekend. Any safety tips?"*
- **Why:** Shows the AI persona's balanced tone and "Actionable Advice" instruction.
- **Expected Insight:** AI provides data-driven context and specific safety recommendations.
